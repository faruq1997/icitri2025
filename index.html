About

Welcome to the 2025 International Conference on Information Technology Research and Innovation (ICITRI). This prestigious event is a hub for sharing groundbreaking research and knowledge in computer and information science. Join us to connect with leading researchers and industry experts, and explore the latest advancements in the field. Note: There is no guarantee that the content will be published in Xplore® and indexed in Scopus.

Conference Topic

Our main theme for ICITRI 2025 is "Harnessing Intelligent Machines for Sustainable Development: Aligning AI with the SDGs". Dive into discussions on how AI can drive sustainable development and align with global goals.

Guidelines

AUTHOR GUIDELINE

Please prepare your manuscripts according to the IEEE Conference Paper format. The manuscript should be 4 to 6 pages long. Research paper should explore a specific technology problem and propose a complete solution to it, with experimental results.

Download Template
ICITRI POLICY

Your submission will be blind-reviewed by at least three independent reviewers. The decision on your paper will be informed via email by the dates listed in the Home section. No guarantee that the content will be published in Xplore® and indexed in Scopus.

NO-SHOW POLICY

The accepted papers that are not presented in the conference will not be included in the final Proceeding and it will not be submitted for inclusion into IEEE Xplore.
